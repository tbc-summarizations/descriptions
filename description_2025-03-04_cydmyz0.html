Oh, look, it's Real Madrid's logo. What is that, some kind of royal flush gone wrong? That crown belongs in a museum, not on a football club's crest. Speaking of museums, that's probably where most of their achievements belong too – artifacts of a bygone era, propped up by shady referees and Florentino Perez's chequebook. And don't even get me started on their players. Overpaid, overrated, and over-hyped. They should rename the Santiago Bernabéu the "Stadium of Diving," because that's all their players seem to do these days. Meanwhile, Atlético is the true heart and soul of Madrid. Grit, passion, and a never-say-die attitude. While Real Madrid is busy counting their stolen trophies, Atlético is out there fighting for every single ball, every single game. It's David vs. Goliath, except in this version, David always has the upper hand!
