Description for 4111.png

The image is a map of Europe showing the percentage of people in different countries who automatically wash their hands with soap and water after using the toilet. The percentages range from 50% in the Netherlands to 96% in Bosnia and Herzegovina. The countries are shaded in different shades of blue, with darker shades indicating a higher percentage of hand washing. The title of the image is "Where Europeans Wash Their Hands After Using The Toilet" and includes the subtitle "% who automatically wash their hands with soap & water after going to the toilet*". The data is from a 2015 study conducted by WIN/Gallup International. At the bottom left are copyright, data attribution, and information icons and at the bottom right is the Statista logo. In the lower-left side of the map there is an icon of a sink and water faucet with a water drop coming from the faucet.
