Description for 1024px-Hungarian_Football_Federation_logo.png

The image is the logo of the Magyar Labdarúgó Szövetség, the Hungarian Football Federation. It's a circular design featuring the Hungarian flag's red and white stripes in the background, along with a white patriarchal cross (the Cross of Lorraine) and a green stylized hill with three peaks. In the foreground, there's a golden soccer ball. Encircling the central elements is a green border with the text "MAGYAR LABDARÚGÓ SZÖVETSÉG" in golden letters at the top and the year "1901" at the bottom, separated by golden dots. The entire logo is outlined with a thin golden border. The overall design is a combination of national symbolism and football elements.
