Here's a summary of how you can and can't use the B035 Scart+VGA+AV to HDMI converter, based on the image: You CAN use it to: • Convert Scart, VGA, and AV signals to HDMI. • Connect a device with Scart, VGA, or AV output to displays with HDMI input (HDTV, Monitor). • Display 720P and 1080P resolutions on a device which support the format 720P or 1080P You CANNOT use it to: • Convert HDMI to Scart. It's a one-way (Scart/VGA/AV to HDMI) converter. • Use a device with RGB output over Scart. It doesn't support Scart RGB mode. • Improve the video quality of your display through scaling • Use a 720P or 1080P with a display that does not support those formats • Use it without an external 5V 1A power supply. You need to power it before connecting video source and display.
