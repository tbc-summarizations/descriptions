H.W Cheating Ethical Dilemma

Whether or not you should cheat on your homework is a personal decision with ethical considerations. While it might seem like a quick way to get it done, there could be consequences like not understanding the material or facing penalties if caught. It might be better to try understanding the concepts and doing the work yourself, or maybe asking for help from your teacher or classmates.
