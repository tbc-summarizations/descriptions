Description for Grb_fudbalske_reprezentacije_Srbije.png

The image is the coat of arms of Serbia. It features a red shield bordered in gold, with the Serbian word "СРБИЈА" (Srbija, meaning Serbia) written in gold Cyrillic letters at the top. Dominating the shield is a white double-headed eagle, also bordered in gold, with silver tongues. The eagle holds two gold fleur-de-lis at the bottom. On the eagle's chest is a smaller red shield containing a white Serbian cross, with four stylized firesteels (or "ocila") in each corner, resembling the Cyrillic letter "С". The overall design is rich in historical and national symbolism, reflecting Serbian heritage and identity.
