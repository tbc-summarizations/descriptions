Description for AEK_Athens_FC_logo.svg.png

The image is a crest for AEK F.C., a Greek sports club. It features a yellow background with a black double-headed eagle, a symbol often associated with Byzantine heritage. Above the eagle are the Greek letters "A.E.K," which stand for "Athlitiki Enosis Konstantinoupoleos" (Athletic Union of Constantinople). "F.C." is printed below the eagle. The crest is framed by a white border.
