Description for 800px-UEFA_Euro_2024_Logo.png

The image is the official logo for UEFA EURO 2024, which will be held in Germany. The logo features a silver trophy positioned in front of a circular background filled with colored stripes representing the participating nations. Below the trophy, "UEFA" is written in an arched formation, followed by "EURO2024" and "GERMANY" in a bold, blue sans-serif font. A small "TM" symbol is placed to the right of the trophy, indicating trademark protection. The overall design is clean and modern, aiming to represent the unity and diversity of European football.
